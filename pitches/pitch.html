02 / The Problem

Managing yield on Solana is broken.

P.01

Fragmented across protocols

Yield lives across Jupiter, Kamino, Drift and others. No single view of where your capital sits or what it earns.

P.02

Desktop-first, every time

Every protocol is built for desktop. On mobile, you get clunky browser tabs and a bad approval flow.

P.03

No real security on mobile

Multi-sig and hardware support effectively don't exist on phones. Hot wallets only - even for whale-size positions.

P.04

Painful position switching

Withdraw → find new site → deposit. Every time a better APY shows up. Most users never bother.
